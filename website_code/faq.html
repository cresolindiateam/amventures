BEFORE TREATMENT INFO
  • Avoid blood thinners & ensure you are hydrated
  • 3 days before avoid topical products such as Tretinoion (Retin-A), Retinols, Glycolic Acid
  • Avoid waxing, threading, bleaching or use of hair removal cream on area being treated
  • Inform aesthetician if you have a history of cold sores
  • Do NOT receive microcurrent or microderm infusion if you are pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Inform aesthetician if you are on, or have previously taken Accutane
AFTER TREATMENT INFO
  • Do NOT apply makeup directly after treatment, wait minimum 3 hours
  • Avoid using any Vitamin A, glycolic or Retinol products for minimum 3 days
  • Avoid extended UV exposure until any redness/swelling has subsided. Be sure to apply SPF 30 or higher for the next week minimum

how long until i start seeing a result ?

Unfortunately, we cannot give you an exact time frame of how long it will take for your skin to clear as every skin type and concern is different.

Can I have microneedling or a chemical peel on my first appointment ?

Unfortunately, no, we always need to prep the skin with in clinic treatments and home care to ensure you get the best results possible. Your aesthetician will advise you of what will best revise & prep your skin.

can i get a facial if i am pregnant or breast feeding ?

Yes, absolutely ask our office admin or aesthetician what facials are safe for you. We also stop all use of topical Vitamin A for our clients during this period.

How long should I come in before my wedding ?

As soon as possible, we want to ensure we can get you the best results possible before your big day.

Can I have a treatment if I have a cold sore ?

No sadly we cannot treat any client with an active cold sore & will have to reschedule the appointment for health and safety reasons.

I am taking acutane can I still have treatments ?

Yes, we can still treat you regardless, please inform your therapist of any medication changes and we can change your skincare treatment plan.
